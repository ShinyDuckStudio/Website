More than 30 unique puzzles divided into three zones!





Zone 1

A part of the forest that is open and easily accessible. This area is where you can encounter hikers. The trees allow sunlight to filter through, giving this zone a calm and peaceful ambiance





Zone 2

This zone, on the other hand, is more obscure. The reason? Forest creatures hunt down any life form that ventures into their territory. With a more autumnal appearance, this zone exudes subtle but possibly imminent danger lurking behind every tree




Zone 3

You might be the first to set foot in this place. With a much darker appearance, you can only enter this zone by venturing deep into the forest. The absurd quantity of monsters will likely complicate your progress toward your goal
